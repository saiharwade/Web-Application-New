ABOUT THIS APPLICATION
D-Vision Pro is an AI-powered diamond segregation system developed by Mindron Meditech India. It integrates with a physical scanning machine to capture real-time images of diamonds and automatically classify them into one of three categories using computer vision and machine learning. The system is designed for diamond labs, grading facilities, and manufacturing units that require fast, accurate, and consistent classification at scale.
CLASSIFICATION
🟢
NATURAL
Diamonds formed deep within the Earth over billions of years under extreme heat and pressure. Mined from kimberlite pipes and alluvial deposits worldwide.
🔵
CVD
Chemical Vapour Deposition — lab-grown diamonds produced by depositing carbon atoms onto a substrate in a vacuum chamber using hydrocarbon gases.
🟠
HPHT
High Pressure High Temperature — lab-grown diamonds created by mimicking the natural formation conditions using extreme pressure and temperature.
